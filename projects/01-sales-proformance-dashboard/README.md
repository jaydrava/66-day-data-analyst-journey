# Sales Performance Dashboard

## Overview
An interactive Excel dashboard analyzing TechMart Electronics sales performance across regions, products, and salespeople for 2025. Built to quickly answer what’s driving revenue and where performance is changing over time.

## What’s Included
- KPI summary: Total Revenue, Total Orders, Average Order Value, Total Quantity  
- Region breakdown: revenue across North, South, East, and West  
- Product performance: top products by revenue and quantity  
- Salesperson performance: individual metrics and ranking  
- Monthly trend: sales by month with a trendline  
- Slicers: Region, Product Category, Salesperson

## Key Insights
- Total Revenue: **$84,109**  
- Top Region: **East ($25,432)**  
- Best Month: **February ($17,780)**  

## Tools Used
Microsoft Excel, PivotTables, charts (bar/line/combo), slicers, conditional formatting, formulas
